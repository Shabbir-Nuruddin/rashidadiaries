# Reel Outro CTA — spoken scripts for Farida

Short, honest closing lines you can film once and add to the end of reels (or post as a
standalone "work with me" reel). They point brands and viewers to the website in your bio.
Everything here is provable from your real numbers — nothing inflated.

> **Setup:** point the link in your Instagram bio to the new website. End the reel by saying
> one of these while a caption on screen reads **"Brand? Tap the link in my bio 🤝"**.

---

## Option A — warm & personal (~15 sec)
"Hi, I'm Farida — the face behind The Rashida Diaries. For years I've been sharing real
family life here in Dubai, and along the way I've partnered with brands like HONOR, Pampers
and Papa John's. If you'd love your brand in front of my community, everything you need —
my reach, my numbers, and how to reach me — is on the link in my bio. Come say hi."

## Option B — confident & direct (~12 sec)
"Eighty thousand of you show up here every week — and brands have noticed. I've created
content that's been watched millions of times for companies across the UAE. If you're a
brand that wants results like that, tap the link in my bio and let's talk."

## Option C — quick & punchy (~8 sec)
"Loved this? So do the brands I work with. Everything about partnering with me — my stats
and my contact — is one tap away in my bio. See you there."

---

## On-screen caption ideas (text overlay)
- "Brands 👉 link in bio"
- "Want your brand here? Tap my bio 🤝"
- "80K community · millions of views · your brand next?"

## Where the link goes
Your bio link should open the media-kit site. Once it's live you'll have a real URL
(for example `therashidadiaries.com` or the free Vercel address). Paste that into:
**Instagram → Edit profile → Links → Add external link.**

## Tips
- Film the outro **once**, in good light, and reuse it. Consistency builds recognition.
- Keep it to a single clear ask: *tap the link in my bio*.
- Mention 2–3 recognisable brands by name — social proof does the persuading for you.
